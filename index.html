About Us

Femprish is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting gender equality and empowerment for all individuals. Our mission is to challenge stereotypes, advocate for equal opportunities, and create a more inclusive society.

Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we strive to create positive change and eliminate the negative connotations associated with the term 'feminist.' Our focus is on driving social, economic, and cultural transformation in India.
